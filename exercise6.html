Halo Infinite

Halo Infinite is an upcoming first-person shooter video game co-developed by 343 Industries and SkyBox Labs, published by Xbox Game Studios for Microsoft Windows, Xbox One and fourth-generation Xbox consoles. The game is scheduled to be released in 2020 and is the sixth main entry of the Halo series.It continues the story of the Master Chief as the third chapter of the "Reclaimer Saga", following Halo 5: Guardians and will also be the sixth game in the franchise with Master Chief as the protagonist.
